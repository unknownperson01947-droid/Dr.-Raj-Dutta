The Physician

Dr. Raj Dutta
MD Medicine

Dr. Raj Dutta is a highly skilled physician with a distinguished specialisation in Diabetes and Critical Care, practising at Dispur Hospital in Assam. Over 15 years, he has built a reputation for clinical excellence paired with genuine compassion.

His expertise spans a broad spectrum of internal medicine, with particular focus on tropical fevers, infectious diseases, and metabolic conditions that are prevalent across North-East India.

Dr. Dutta believes that exceptional medical care is rooted in listening — understanding each patient's full picture before crafting a personalised treatment pathway.
